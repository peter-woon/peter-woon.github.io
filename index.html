Peter Woon is a highly experienced digital designer with over 10 years of experience in the field. He is a specialist in digital banner production and animation. His knowledge in design technology includes Google Web Designer, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. He has worked on projects for some of the biggest names in the industry, including Emirates, Honda Kah Motors, Standard Chartered Bank, Singapore Airlines and TigerAir. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating engaging digital experiences, Peter is an asset to any team.

Peter is keen to learn more about interactive generative arts and motion graphics. In his free time, Peter dabbles in DJing, Bouldering and supports The Arsenal Football Club from the English Premier League.
